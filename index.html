About Me

I was born and raised in Dhaka, Bangladesh. I have done my Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from BUET. My undergraduate thesis was on machine learning of time series dataset.
I have been involved in volunteering for the last four years. I have served as the General Secretary at IEEE BUET Student Branch.
I have been an active musician for quite some time now. My main instrument is the drums. I also play other percussions, guitar, and a little bit of keyboard. I have been playing with my band Oddity Spell since 2018.
I am an intermediate programmer an a novice hacker.
Other than all these, I am a tech nerd and a casual gamer. I sometimes stream on my YouTube channel.
